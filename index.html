Transformer Architecture

The transformer architecture is the basis for all the modern llms.

Generations

Llama is the first generation of the family of llama models which were leaked on March 3, 2023.

Llama2 is the second generation of the family of llama models which were released on August 24, 2023.

Llama3 is the third generation of the family of llama models which were released on April 18, 2024.
